About us:

Each year, millions of tourists travel all over the world to enjoy both the best beaches and most spectacular wildlife that lies offshore. Unintentionally, these tourists are destroying an underwater world that is vital to the ocean's ecosystem. The main contributors to this disaster are oxybenzone and octinoxate. These substances contain nanoparticles that have shown to lead to bleaching by destabilizing coral’s reproductive system and growth.

Eco-Screen is an environmentally friendly sunscreen product designed to protect travelers from the sun and protect coral reefs from the travelers’ sunscreen. Instead of using the harmful coral-killing chemicals to protect tourists from the sun, our products utilize zinc oxide and titanium dioxide. These non-nano particles chemicals are mineral-based and cannot be ingested by the reefs. We are doing our part by helping tourists do their part by not harming coral reefs around the world.
